OPERATING SYSTEM

Skill is very important in the life of every human being. The reason why many technicians earn more money than some university graduates is because we acquire more practical skills. Skill acquisition is the ability to be trained on a particular task or function and become an expert in it.
A skilled person can survive in any environment.
As water is essential to human life, so is skill training and acquisition needed in the life of every serious minded human being.

REASONS FOR SKILL AQUISITION

People may acquire different skills for the following reasons
  1. As survival strategy: people acquire certain skills in order to make a living out of it and for survival.
  2. Managing emergency situation: some people may learn some skills to enable them handle emergency situations
  3. To be self-reliant: some people learn skill in order to be self reliant. They may not want to work for government or private employers.

IMPORTANCE OF SKILL ACQUISITION

  • Skill acquisition creates employment opportunities
  • Skill acquisition makes one to be self-reliant and improves the quality of life of an individual.
  • Skill acquisition can reduce high crime rate of unemployment by occupying young people who could have channeled their energies into antisocial behavior.
  • Skill acquisition enables people to contribute meaningfully to the economic and social development of the nation through their good services.

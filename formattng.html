Types of computer


Analog computer
Digital computer
Hybrid computer

Analog computer is the type of computer that processes data into physical quantities. Examples of analog computer is Speedometer, Thermometer, etc

Digital computer converts data into binary format. This format comes in zeros and ones (00001111). Examples are Wrist wath, PC etc
Hybrid computer is the combination of both analog and digital computer to process data.
